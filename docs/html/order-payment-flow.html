GoPostal SD — Order & Payment Flow

How a customer order moves from checkout to fulfillment and payout

The Flow

  1. Customer places an order directly on the GoPostal SD web application.
  2. GoPostal sends the order to Sinalite — our wholesale print partner receives the order request.
  3. Sinalite quotes wholesale price — Sinalite charges its wholesale cost for production and fulfillment.
  4. GoPostal calculates the retail price — wholesale + GoPostal profit margin (%) + tax.
    Amount Due = Wholesale + (Wholesale × Markup %) + Tax
  5. Customer sees and pays the final price — the total amount due is shown at checkout and collected via payment processing.
  6. Funds are split behind the scenes — on successful payment, GoPostal keeps its profit %, sends wholesale cost to Sinalite, and sends tax uncle Sam.
