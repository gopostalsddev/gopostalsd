# Page Suggestions for Go Postal SD

## Current Pages
1. **Home** (`/`) - Company introduction and services
2. **Shop** (`/shop`) - Product catalog and shopping
3. **Contact** (`/contact`) - Contact information
4. **Cart** (`/cart`) - Shopping cart
5. **Checkout** (`/checkout`) - Order checkout
6. **Auth Pages** - Login, Register, Password Reset, etc.
7. **Admin** (`/admin`) - Admin dashboard
8. **Order Confirmation** (`/order-confirmation`) - Order success page

## Suggested Additional Pages

### 1. **Services Page** (`/services`)
- Detailed list of all printing services offered
- Service categories (Business Cards, Flyers, Posters, etc.)
- Pricing information and turnaround times
- Service descriptions and use cases
- Could link to specific products in the shop

### 2. **Gallery/Portfolio Page** (`/gallery`)
- Showcase of completed projects
- Before/after examples
- Different print types and finishes
- Customer testimonials with photos
- Builds trust and credibility

### 3. **FAQs Page** (`/faqs`)
- Common questions about printing services
- Order process questions
- Pricing questions
- File upload requirements
- Turnaround time information
- Helps reduce support requests

### 4. **Account/Profile Page** (`/account`)
- User account management
- Order history
- Saved addresses
- Payment methods
- Notification preferences
- Change password
- Account settings

### 5. **Order Tracking Page** (`/track-order` or `/orders`)
- View order status
- Track shipments
- Download invoices/receipts
- Reorder items
- Contact support about specific orders

### 6. **Blog/News Page** (`/blog` or `/news`)
- Printing tips and tricks
- Industry news
- Design inspiration
- Special offers and announcements
- Help with SEO and content marketing

### 7. **Special Offers Page** (`/specials` or `/offers`)
- Current promotions
- Seasonal deals
- Bulk discounts
- Loyalty program information
- Coupon codes
- Limited-time offers

### 8. **File Upload Guidelines Page** (`/upload-guidelines`)
- File format requirements
- Image resolution guidelines
- Bleed and trim specifications
- Color profile information
- File naming conventions
- Upload tips and best practices

### 9. **Terms & Conditions Page** (`/terms`)
- Terms of service
- Privacy policy
- Return/refund policy
- Shipping terms
- Payment terms
- Copyright information

### 10. **Locations/Hours Page** (`/location`)
- Store location and map
- Store hours
- Contact information
- Parking information
- Directions
- Virtual tour (optional)

### 11. **Custom Orders Page** (`/custom-orders`)
- Request quotes for custom printing
- Upload files for review
- Description of special services
- Turnaround times for custom work
- Contact form for custom requests

### 12. **Wholesale/Business Page** (`/wholesale` or `/business`)
- Information for business customers
- Bulk pricing
- Account setup for businesses
- Tax-exempt information
- Custom delivery options
- Business account benefits

## Priority Recommendations

### High Priority
1. **Services Page** - Helps customers find what they need
2. **FAQs Page** - Reduces support load
3. **Upload Guidelines** - Prevents order errors
4. **Account Page** - Essential for user experience

### Medium Priority
5. **Order Tracking** - Important for customer satisfaction
6. **Gallery** - Builds trust and showcases quality
7. **File Upload Guidelines** - Helps users prepare correct files

### Lower Priority (Nice to Have)
8. **Blog/News** - Content marketing and SEO
9. **Special Offers** - Promotions and marketing
10. **Custom Orders** - For specialized requests
11. **Wholesale Page** - If targeting business customers

## Quick Implementation Ideas

### Easy to Implement
- **FAQs**: Static content with accordions
- **File Upload Guidelines**: Lists and tables
- **Location/Hours**: Contact info and Google Maps embed

### Medium Difficulty
- **Services**: List with cards, links to shop
- **Gallery**: Image carousel/grid with lightbox
- **Account Page**: User data display and forms

### More Complex
- **Order Tracking**: Requires order history from backend
- **Blog**: Requires CMS or backend content management
- **Special Offers**: Dynamic pricing and coupon system

## Navigation Suggestions

Add to main navigation:
- **Services** (high traffic page)
- **FAQs** (help page)

Consider footer for:
- Terms & Conditions
- Privacy Policy
- Upload Guidelines

User menu (when logged in):
- **My Orders** (instead of separate account page initially)
- **My Account** (expand later)
